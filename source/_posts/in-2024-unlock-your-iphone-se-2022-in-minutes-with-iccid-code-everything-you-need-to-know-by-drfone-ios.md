This enables mobile carriers and networks to recognize and authenticate the Apple iPhone SE (2022) device.

The ICCID code is essential for your Apple iPhone SE (2022) to connect smoothly to cellular networks. When you put a SIM card in your Apple iPhone SE (2022), the Apple iPhone SE (2022) device reads the ICCID code. This code helps identify the carrier, subscription details, and network services.
