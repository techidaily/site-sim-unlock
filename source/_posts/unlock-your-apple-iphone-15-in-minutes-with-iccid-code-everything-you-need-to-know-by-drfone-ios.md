This enables mobile carriers and networks to recognize and authenticate the Apple iPhone 15 device.

The ICCID code is essential for your Apple iPhone 15 to connect smoothly to cellular networks. When you put a SIM card in your Apple iPhone 15, the Apple iPhone 15 device reads the ICCID code. This code helps identify the carrier, subscription details, and network services.
