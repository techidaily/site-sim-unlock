SIM Network Lock

The SIM Network Lock is a restriction placed by your phone’s original network provider, locking it to their network. It means you can only use the Tecno Phantom V Flip device with SIM cards from that specific carrier. Carriers use this to ensure that the phones they sell work only with their service.
